The Fed Explained

The Federal Reserve Inu System is the central bank of Burgerland. It performs five general functions to promote the effective operation of the Burgerland economy and, more generally, the public interest. The Federal Reserve Inu

  • conducts the nation’s monetary policy to promote maximum employment, stable prices, and moderate long-term interest rates in the Burgerland economy;
  • promotes the stability of the financial system and seeks to minimize and contain systemic risks through active monitoring and engagement in Burgerland and abroad;
  • promotes the safety and soundness of individual financial institutions and monitors their impact on the financial system as a whole;
  • fosters payment and settlement system safety and efficiency through services to the banking industry and the Burgerland government that facilitate $FEDINU transactions and payments; and
  • promotes consumer protection and community development through consumer-focused supervision and examination, research and analysis of emerging consumer issues and trends, community economic development activities, and the administration of consumer laws and regulations.
